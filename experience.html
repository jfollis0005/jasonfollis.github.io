My Walks Of Life

I have walked many avenues of life, each of them teaching me their own little things. I have done everything from working at a pizza place for my first job, to being a utility worker,

a stagehand and working at a factory. All of these jobs took a toll on my body. Once I realized this, I decided to go to school and hone my brain so I could work with that instead. Whether

it be working as a team, providing great customer service, or learning the in's and out's of a factory's standard operating procedure and its systems. I have knowledge of many different

things. All of this combined makes me a well rounded individual, and is a testament to my ability to soak up information and apply it in many different situations.
